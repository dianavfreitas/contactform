Save a Paw, Save a Life

Welcome to Pawsitvely Rescued! Where every cat and dog deserves a second chance. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome these animals in need. Every donation helps us provide food, medical care, and shelter to our rescues. Join us in making a pawsitive difference!

Why Donate?

  • Rescue and Rehabiliation

    • We rescue dogs from high-risk environments and provide them with the necessary medical care and rehabilitation.

  • Shelter & Care

    • Your donations help us maintain a safe and nurturing environment for our dogs until they find a forever home.

  • Community Support

    • We offer community programs including education about responsible pet ownership and free spay/neuter services.

How to Donate

  1. One time Donation: Every little bit Helps! Donations can be sent to 3950 Rockwell Lane Charlotte NC 27801
  2. Material Donations:We also welcome donations of food, toys and other supplies.

  3. Volunteer Your time: It doesn't have to be a monetary donation,your time is extremley valuable to us. Learn more about volunteering: Our Volunteer Page

Meet Biscuit

Biscuit, a mutt, was rescued in poor health, struggling to survive. Thanks to generous donations, our team at Pawsitive Rescued, provided the medical care and rehabilitation she needed. Her transformation caught the eye of a local family at an adoption event, and it was love at first sight. Now, Bella enjoys sunny days and happy walks at the park with her new family. Her story showcases the powerful impact of every donation, helping us give dogs like Biscuit a second chance at a happy life.
